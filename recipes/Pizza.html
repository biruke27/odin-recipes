Pizza

Description

Pizza is a flat bread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and any toppings you like, baked until the cheese melts and the crust is golden. It’s fun to make at home and loved by everyone.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a baking tray.
  2. Add toppings: Spread tomato sauce on the dough, sprinkle cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  3. Bake: Cook in an oven at 200°C (400°F) for 12–15 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden.
  4. Serve: Slice and enjoy hot!
