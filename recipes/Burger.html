Burger

Description

A burger is a sandwich with a cooked meat patty inside a soft bun, often topped with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and sauce. Itâ€™s quick, filling, and can be customized any way you like.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground beef (or chicken)
  • Cheese slices
  • Onion & garlic
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (optional)
  • Ketchup or mayonnaise
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Make the patty: Mix ground meat with salt and pepper, shape into patties.
  2. Cook the patty: Fry or grill patties until fully cooked.
  3. Assemble the burger: Put the patty on the bun, add cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and sauce.
  4. Serve: Put the top bun on and enjoy!
